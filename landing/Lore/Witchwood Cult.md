# The Witchwood Cult

A patron-only page — readers at the `public` tier won't see this article at
all (it doesn't appear in search, sitemap, or backlinks for them, and a
direct URL 404s). At the `patron` tier and above, it renders normally.

## What's known

The cult takes recruits from travellers along the Witchwood Road, usually
the kind no one will miss. They wear bone-bleached masks during their
ceremonies and never seem to leave tracks.

> [!dm] DM only
> They're a splinter of the older Order of the Pale Crown. Their goal is
> to reawaken something buried under the [[Hidden Caves]]. The masks are
> stitched from finger bones — one per recruit, taken before initiation.
>
> Their founding text — "On Buried Things" — was written by a younger
> [[Dr. Bixby Wizzlethorpe]] as an academic curiosity. He has no idea
> they exist.

## Confirmed casualties

- Aelar's sister Liriel (3 winters ago).
- Two travelling merchants, summer last.
- A Balandel courier whose body was returned headless.
